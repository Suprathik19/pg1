Welcome to Sri vasavi PG

We are dedicated to providing a peaceful and friendly living space for students and working professionals. Our focus is on creating an environment that feels safe, hygienic, and homely, so you can concentrate on your studies or work without worries..

Our property offers well-maintained rooms, reliable power backup, high-speed WiFi, and clean food facilities to meet your daily needs. We believe good food and a clean living space are essential for a healthy lifestyle, which is why we provide home-cooked meals, daily room cleaning, and hot water facilities throughout the year..
